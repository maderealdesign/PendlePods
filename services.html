Common Questions

Services FAQs

Do all your services include full insulation for year-round use?

Yes, regardless of whether you choose a garden office, gym, bar, or workshop, every Pendle Pod is built to house-grade standards with premium PIR rigid insulation wrapped seamlessly across the floor, walls, and roof.

Do you manage the entire project from start to finish?

Absolutely. We offer a true turnkey service. From initial design and eco-friendly ground screw foundations to final plastering, premium flooring, and electrical certification, our in-house team handles every single aspect of the build.

Can I combine different services into one building?

Yes! Because every pod is custom-designed, we frequently build multi-purpose spaces. For example, we can design a single large building with an internal partition wall separating a quiet home office from an energetic home gym or bar area.

Are your timber frame extensions faster than traditional brick extensions?

Significantly faster. Because our timber frames are prefabricated off-site, the structural shell of a home extension can be erected and made watertight in a matter of days, drastically reducing mess and disruption to your daily life compared to traditional wet-trade brick builds.
