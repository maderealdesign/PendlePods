The Quality Inside

Anatomy of a Pendle Pod

What you see in our gallery is only half the story. The true value of a Pendle Pod lies in the premium materials and house-grade construction methods beneath the surface.

House-Grade Insulation

Thick PIR rigid insulation boards wrap the floor, walls, and ceiling, ensuring unparalleled thermal efficiency year-round.

Premium Glazing

High-security, highly efficient double glazing in uPVC or sleek Aluminum frames, filling your space with natural light.

Flawless Interiors

Fully skimmed plasterboard walls, professionally painted, finished with high-quality skirting and durable laminate flooring.

Weatherproof Roofs

Single-piece Firestone EPDM rubber roofing ensures a 100% watertight seal with an expected lifespan of over 50 years.

Design & Options FAQs

Can I replicate a specific design from your gallery?

Yes, absolutely. If you see a specific style, cladding type, or layout in our gallery that you love, we can replicate it or use it as a starting point to tailor a design specifically for your garden's dimensions and your personal needs.

Are the interiors finished exactly as seen in the pictures?

Yes, all our Silver, Gold, and Premium tier pods come fully 'turnkey'. This means the smooth plastered walls, painted finishes, high-quality flooring, skirting boards, and electrical fittings you see in our gallery are completely standard and included in your quoted price.

Do you offer different exterior cladding options?

We offer a wide variety of premium exterior finishes. As seen across our portfolio, popular choices include naturally stunning Canadian Western Red Cedar, pressure-treated Scandinavian Redwood, highly durable low-maintenance composite cladding, and crisp, modern rendered finishes to match modern homes.

Can I visit a showsite to see a pod in person?

While our online gallery provides a comprehensive look at our craftsmanship, we completely understand the desire to feel the quality in person. Please contact us, and we can arrange a viewing of a completed local project or invite you to visit our workshop during the construction phase of a current build.
